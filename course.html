Facilities & Services

At Mia University, we strive to provide a comprehensive and well-equipped campus for our students, faculty, and staff. Our facilities and services include:

World Class Library

Our library offers a wealth of academic resources, including books, magazines, and e-books. We also have a state-of-the-art computer lab, allowing students to work on projects and research.

Biggest Play ground

Our basketball courts are the perfect place for students to practice their skills, socialize, and enjoy a great game. We also have a state-of-the-art basketball equipment, such as hoops, nets, and hoop cleats.

Healthy & Well-rounded Food

Our cafeteria offers a variety of healthy, nutritious, and delicious food options, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We also have a state-of-the-art kitchen, allowing students to prepare their own meals.
